How much is an annuity worth at the end of three years if it pays $100 at the end of each year assuming 10% interest?

First, identify the variables. Then plug the numbers into the equation. Remember to work the equation according to the order of operations. The total agrees with our calculation of Future Value for each individual lump sum payment.

\(FVA=CF[\frac{(1+i)^{n}-1}i]\)
\(FVA=CF[\frac{(1+.10)^{3}-1}{.10}]\)
\(FVA=CF[\frac{(1.331)-1}{.10}]\)
\(FVA=CF[\frac{.331}{.10}]\)
\(FVA=\$331\)
