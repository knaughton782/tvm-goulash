You should also know how to use the TVM buttons on your financial calculators to find PVA:

FV is 0 because, though there are regularly scheduled payments, there is no lump sum paid at the end of 3 years. If there were a lump sum paid at the end of the term, it would be positive because, as usual, PV and FV are opposite signs (+/-).

Always make sure your financial calculator is on the END mode. This is the most cmomon mistake made on exams.
