TVM: Present Value of Annuities

Exercise 12: Solution

Is this question about an annuity, lump sum, or a combination of the two?

What would you pay for a bond that pays $100 at the end of each year and the $1000 face value at the end of 6 years, if you want to earn a 5% rate of return?

Answer:
C) Combination (PVA and PV)

The annual $100 payments are an annuity. The face value paid at the end of 5 years is a lump sum. You must calculate the PV of the annuity and lump sum separately, and then add them together to determine what you should pay today.
