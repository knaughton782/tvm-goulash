Financial Calculator

Also attempt to answer the problem with the TVM buttons on your financial calculator.

If you borrow $20,000 to finance a car purchase at a rate of 9% (stated nominal rate) for a 48-month note, how much will the monthly car payment be?

Note that PV is positive because you received the $20,000 now. PMT is negative because it is a monthly outgoing cash flow.
