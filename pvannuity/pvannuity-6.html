Now let's use the PVA equation with compounding.

\(PVA=CF[\frac{1-\frac{1}{(1+\frac{i}{m})^{n\cdot m}}}{\frac{i}{m}}]\)

Answer this question:

A monthly car payment is by definition an ordinary annuity, because you pay equal-sized payments at the end of every month for a specific number of months. If you borrow $20,000 to finance a car purchase at a rate of 9% (stated nominal rate) for a 48-month note, how much will the monthly car payment be?

Remember the steps to solve the equation.

  • Identify the variables
  • Input the variables into the equation
  • Solve the equation using the order of operations

The answer is CF = $497.70
