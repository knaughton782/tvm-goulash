Present Value of Annuities (PVA)

The Present Value of Annuities (PVA) answers the question:

How much would I pay today for a stream of cash flows over a finite period of time at a specific interest rate?

Finding the Present Value is know as discounting, and its the reverse of compounding.

Before finding PVA, you might find it helpful to review how to calculate PV (discount) using the basic TVM formula.
