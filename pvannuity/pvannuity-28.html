Exercise 9: Solution

Is this question about an annuity, lump sum, or a combination of the two?

What is the monthly payment for a 30-year mortgage on a house that costs $200,000 if you put down $10,000 and pay 5% interest?

Answer:
A) Annuity (PVA)

A mortgage is an annuity. Since there are no additional lump sum payments, this is a straightforward annuity problem using the PVA equation.
