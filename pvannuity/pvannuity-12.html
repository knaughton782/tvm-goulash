Present Value of Annuities (PVA)

The PVA we just calculated is for an ordinary annuity. Remember that the difference between ordinary annuities and annuities due is the timing of payments:

Ordinary Annuity - Payments are made at the end of each period

Annuity Due - Payments are made at the beginning of each period

You may remember from TVM3, when we first introduced annuities, that finding the value of an ordinary annuity and an annuity due are exactly the same except for one crucial step at the end:


Ordinary Annuity
  • Calculate the PV using the PVA equation

Annuity Due
  • Calculate the PV using the PVA equation
  • Add an extra period of interest to the PVA amount using the PV equation.

    where PV = PVA
