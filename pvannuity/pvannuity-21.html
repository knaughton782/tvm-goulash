TVM: Annuities

Present Value of Annuities (PVA)

Solving a combination problem follows basically the same path, though it may require more calculation. The steps are always the same.

  • Determine whether the problem involves an annuity, a lump sum, or a combination of the two.
  • Determine which variables you do and don't know: interest rate, number of years, compounding and CF frequency, amount of the CFs, PV amount, or FV amount.
  • Solve for the unknown variable(s).
