Exercise 1: Calculate the FV of an Annuity Due

What if I deposited the $1200 a year at the beginning of the year for 20 years earning 8% interest? How much would the account be worth?
(Hint: as an ordinary annuity, it was worth $54,914.36).

To convert from an ordinary annuity to an annuity due, every payment receives an extra period of interest. Use the general form of the TVM equation to add 1 year of interest.
