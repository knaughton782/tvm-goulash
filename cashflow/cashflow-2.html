TVM: Cash Flows

Cash Flows

Cash flows are defined as:

Regularly scheduled, equal payments over the term of the investment or loan.

Cash flows are prevalent in personal and business finance. As a lender, you may receive cash flows in the form of dividends or coupon payments. As a borrower, you may be required to make payments on your car or mortgage. The TVM equation can be modified to account for cash flows, and by adding them, the TVM equation really starts to become useful.
