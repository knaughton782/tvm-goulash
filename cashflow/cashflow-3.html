Although you can find the correct answer by calculating the Future Value of each individual cash flow as demonstrated above, it is a time consuming and inefficient process. Further:

  • Its boring and tedious!
  • Each individual calculation introduces another opportunity to commit a rounding or calculation error.
  • Even if you use a spreadsheet and formulas, questions with really long terms (e.g., a 45 year retirement account) are still unwieldy.
