What is the future value of an account if I deposit $1000 at the start of each year for 10 years at 10% interest?

You could use the formula you already know to calculate the future value of each payment individually and then add them all together, like the image below, until you have the Future Value of all 10 payments. Adding them together results in a Future Value of $17,531.17.

Although you can find the correct answer by calculating the Future Value of each individual cash flow as demonstrated above, it is a time consuming and inefficient process. In addition, each calculation provides an opportunity to commit a calculation error. Even if you use a spreadsheet and formulas, questions with really long terms (e.g., a 45 year retirement account) are still unwieldy.
