Once you have plugged the variables into the equation, solve using the order of operations.

Remember the acronym PEMDAS.

  • Parentheses
  • Exponents
  • Multiplication and Division (left to right)
  • Addition and Subtraction (left to right)

Use PEMDAS to solve the equation:

  • P: Calculate the number within the parentheses.
  • E: Raise the number in the parentheses by the exponent.
  • MD: Multiply the remaining numbers. (AS: There's nothing left to simplify - division, addition, or subtraction). Round to the nearest cent and your done.
